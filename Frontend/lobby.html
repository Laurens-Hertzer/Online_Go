Rules of Go

Goal: Control more territory than your opponent.


Placing stones: Players alternate placing stones on intersections. Black goes first.


Capturing: Surround an opponent's stone or group (remove all liberties) to capture it.


Liberties: Empty adjacent intersections (up, down, left, right) of a stone or group.


Suicide: You cannot place a stone that would have no liberties, unless it captures opponent stones.


Passing: You may pass your turn. If both players pass consecutively, the game ends.


Scoring: Territory (empty points you surround) + captured stones = your score.
